About this application

The goal of this application is to map the extent of damage to vegetation cover after the two major hurricanes in the Caribbean Islands in Sep 2017. This application was built on top of our API using Radiant.Earth's Platform without ever downloading a single sattelite image.

What is Radiant.Earth

Radiant.Earth provides a geospatial and imagery technology platform that supports knowledge transfer to positively impact the developing world’s greatest social, economic and environmental challenges. To learn more, check our website.
